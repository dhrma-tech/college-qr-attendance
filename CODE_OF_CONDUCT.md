# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make ScanRoll a welcoming open-source project for contributors of all experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Help maintain a safe environment for students and educators.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Discrimination
- Publishing private data
- Sharing secrets or credentials

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.
